Proteles — colour themes

Each card is the same Aardwolf output rendered through a complete 16-colour palette + foreground/background. Dark themes are primary; the two light themes are hand-tuned (and clamp "white/bright-white" to dark ink) so MUD text stays legible — the thing Mudlet/MUSHclient never solved. The strip under each title is its 16 ANSI colours (8 normal, 8 bright).
